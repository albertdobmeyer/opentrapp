Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

OpenTrApp collects no personal data. There is no telemetry, no tracking, no analytics, and no account. We have nothing to sell and nothing to leak, because we never receive your information in the first place.

The desktop application

  • No telemetry or analytics. The app does not phone home, does not report usage, crashes, or metrics to us, and contains no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • Everything runs locally. The application and its security perimeter run entirely on your own computer. Your files, your workspace, and your activity stay on your machine.
  • Your credentials stay on your machine. The API key and Telegram bot token you provide during setup are stored locally in your own user profile and are used only to connect to the services you configured. They are never transmitted to the OpenTrApp project.
  • The only external connections are the ones you set up. When you run an agent, it talks to the AI vendor's API (e.g. Anthropic) and to Telegram — services you choose and authenticate with directly. Those providers have their own privacy policies; this policy covers only OpenTrApp itself.

This website

  • The site at opentrapp.com is a static page. It sets no tracking cookies and runs no analytics or advertising scripts.
  • As with any web server, the hosting provider may keep standard, short-lived access logs (such as IP address and requested page) for security and reliability. These are not used to identify or profile you and are not shared.
  • Download links point to the project's GitHub Releases; GitHub's own privacy policy applies once you follow them.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be published on this page with an updated date. Because the project is developed in the open, the full history of this file is visible in the source repository.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be raised as an issue on the GitHub repository.
